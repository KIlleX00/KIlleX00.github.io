This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our iOS applications ("Apps"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Important: By using our Apps, you consent to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account or make purchases, we may collect your email address and other account details

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide technical support
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Advertising and Google AdMob

Our Apps use Google AdMob to display advertisements. Here's what you need to know:

4.1 How AdMob Works

  • AdMob may collect and use your advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS)
  • Ads may be personalized based on your interests and app usage
  • AdMob may use cookies and similar technologies

4.2 Your Ad Choices

  • You can limit ad tracking in your iOS device settings
  • You can reset your advertising identifier in your device settings
  • You can opt out of personalized ads through Google Ad Settings

Note: Even if you opt out of personalized ads, you may still see advertisements, but they will be less relevant to your interests.
